Multi-award winner, “Michelin Guide” recommended, food critics positive reviewed and an absolute top neighborhood favorite, La Superior brings the flavor, the sounds and the experience of Mexico city, in a cozy small vibrant

Established 11 years ago in the summer of 2008, La Superior has become a fixture and staple of Williamsburg Brooklyn.

Brainchild of chef/partner Felipe Mendez-Candelas, a Mexico City native which started as an idea in his apartment in Greenpoint, in the early 2000’s, as a response to the lack of authentic “taquerias” and legit Mexican street food in New York City. Menu is mainly based in his home town’s neighborhood Coyoacan and it’s large street food culture with additions to other Mexico favorites like torta ahogada, cochinita pibil and ceviches.
